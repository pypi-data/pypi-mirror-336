Pricing and payment

Here you can find information regarding pricing and payment.

Pricing

30 Swedish kronor or 3 euro per month for every 1G of total storage. The most total amount of disc space that you used during the 1 month period will be what you will be billed for. The storage calculation will always round up, this means that the smallest amount of storage space that an account will be billed for is 1G.

The first every month we will try to withdraw funds from your account according to your usage previous month. It is recommended that you pay in advance. If there are insufficient funds in your account on the first of the month your account will be disabled and after 15 days, if no payment has been made the account with all its data will be removed.

Payment method

Payment is physical cash. Payment is done by putting your cash and payment token (randomly generated on our website) in an physical envelope and sending it to us. When we recive the money we will add them to your accounts funds and generate a new payment token for your account.
